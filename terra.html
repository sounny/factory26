Context

What is TERRA?

TERRA is a Horizon Europe project developing operational climate services from Copernicus Sentinel satellite data. We translate raw Earth Observation into actionable intelligence for policymakers, farmers, and urban planners.

  • Water Stress Monitoring
  • Forest Health Assessment
  • Coastal Erosion Mapping
  • Agricultural Yield Forecasting
[ Live Demo: Copernicus Browser ]

Pilots

Three Pilot Regions

🇬🇷

Crete, Greece

Water stress monitoring for olive groves and vineyards using NDWI indices from Sentinel-2.

🇫🇮

Finland

Boreal forest health tracking with SAR-based biomass estimation from Sentinel-1.

🇮🇪

Ireland

Coastal erosion and flood risk mapping with multi-temporal change detection.

Impact

From Data to Policy

Work Package 6 translates pilot results into actionable policy recommendations for European and national decision-makers.

  • Stakeholder interviews with 4–6 policymakers
  • Exploitation pathways for commercial spin-off
  • Alignment with EU Green Deal and CAP
  • Open-data principles for reproducibility
