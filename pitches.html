Project 1

🚢 LogiRhein Live

Le Rhin, en direct. The Rhine, live.

An immersive scrollytelling web application that visualizes how Rhine water levels directly impact Europe's most critical inland shipping corridor, right here through Strasbourg.

  • The crisis: In 2018, record low water at the Kaub gauge paralyzed Rhine shipping for months, costing billions
  • The cascade: Low water → reduced cargo → factory shutdowns → consumer price spikes
  • The tool: Interactive gauge simulator, real-time data layers, and economic impact calculator
  • Local connection: "Strasbourg: NOUS SOMMES ICI" on the Rhine corridor map
↗ View Live App

Key Features

  • 3D globe zoom to Rhine corridor
  • Historical crisis timeline (2018 & 2022)
  • Interactive water level gauge
  • Economic cascade visualization
  • Bilingual (FR/EN) with live data
